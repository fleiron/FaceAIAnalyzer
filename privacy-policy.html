Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 4, 2025

Welcome to Face AI Analyzer (the “App”). We respect your privacy and design our features to minimize data collection. This Policy explains what data is accessed, how it is used, and your choices.

1) Who We Are

For any privacy questions, contact: ilushazs@icloud.com.

2) Personal Information

The App does not require you to create an account and does not collect your name, email, phone number, or other personally identifying information.

3) Face Data (Images, Landmarks) — Collection, Use, Storage, Sharing, Retention

Summary: The App accesses your camera or chosen photo to analyze facial features and compute an in-app score. All analysis happens locally on your device. The App does not collect, upload, store, or share your face images or biometric templates with us or any third parties.

3.1 What face data does the App access?

  • Camera frames or a photo you select for analysis
  • Derived, on-device facial landmarks (e.g., general contours, eye/mouth positions)

3.2 How is that data used (planned uses)?

  • To compute aesthetic/appearance metrics and display your FaceScore in the App
  • To enable optional comparisons and history inside the App
  • No advertising or user profiling is performed based on face data

3.3 Storage and Retention

  • Images and biometric templates are not stored or uploaded.
  • Face data is processed in volatile memory and discarded immediately after analysis.
  • If you choose to save history, the App stores only non-biometric values (e.g., numeric score, timestamps, your text notes) locally on your device — not the face image.
  • You may delete this local history at any time by using in-app controls or by deleting the App.

3.4 Sharing with Third Parties

  • No face data is shared with any third parties.
  • We use RevenueCat solely to validate purchases/subscriptions. RevenueCat does not receive face images or facial landmarks. It may receive anonymous purchase events/device identifiers for fraud prevention and entitlement management.

3.5 Where in this Policy is face data explained?

See sections: 3 Face Data, 3.3 Storage and Retention, 3.4 Sharing with Third Parties.

3.6 Key Quote (for App Review)

“The App does not store, upload, or share face images or biometric templates. All facial analysis is processed locally on the user’s device and discarded after computation. If the user saves history, only non-biometric values (e.g., numeric scores) are stored locally.”

4) Technical & Purchase Data

For diagnostics and purchase validation, we may process anonymized technical signals such as device model, app version, crash logs, and in-app purchase events (via RevenueCat). This data is used only to maintain stability, detect fraud, and deliver entitlements. It is not used to identify you personally and is not linked to face data.

5) Push Notifications

The App currently does not send push notifications.

6) Security

We follow industry-standard practices. Because data is primarily on your device and we do not store face images or biometric templates, privacy risks are reduced. Still, no system is 100% secure; please keep your device protected (e.g., passcode/biometric lock).

7) Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8) Your Controls

  • Delete local history in-app or by deleting the App
  • Manage purchases via your Apple ID (Subscriptions)

9) Third-Party Services

RevenueCat (purchase validation & subscriptions). RevenueCat does not receive your face images/landmarks. It may handle anonymous identifiers and purchase metadata for fraud prevention and entitlement management.

10) Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy. The “Last updated” date will reflect the latest version.

11) Contact

Questions? ilushazs@icloud.com
